f311.convmol.downloaders.downnist.get_nist_webbook_constants(formula, flag_unicode=True)¶

Navigates through NIST webbook pages to retrieve a table of molecular constants

Parameters:formula – example: “OH”

Returns: tuple: table (list of lists), header (list of strings), name of molecule

Disclaimer This scraper matches a specific version of the Chemistry Web Book. Therefore, it may stop working if the NIST web site is updated.
